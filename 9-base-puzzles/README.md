# 9 Base Puzzles

The 9 base puzzles from the original [video playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLj-VLkYRjRxm5HVGFVpPP5W7jkvvzd1q7). Each puzzle results in a single word of a specified length.

| #   | Puzzle                     | Word Length | Answer    |
| --- | -------------------------- | ----------- | --------- |
| 1   | [Wells](wells/)            | 5           | EVERY     |
| 2   | [Strangers](stangers/)     | 9           | CHALLENGE |
| 3   | [Beaches](beaches/)        | 5           | LEADS     |
| 4   | [1 vs 500k](1-vs-500k/)    | 7           | TOWARD    |
| 5   | [Pokemon Go](pokemon-go/)  | 8           | LOCATION  |
| 6   | [Tents](tents/)            | 4           | NAME      |
| 7   | [100 Dogs](100-dogs/)      | 9           | SOMEWHERE |
| 8   | [Pyramid](pyramid/)        | 6           | AROUND    |
| 9   | [Sphere](sphere/)          | 5           | WORLD     |

## Combined Answer

> **EVERY CHALLENGE LEADS TOWARD LOCATION NAME SOMEWHERE AROUND WORLD**

Believed to be a hint regarding the information in the 4 videos, corresponding to 50 locations that will reveal the next hint when uncovered.
